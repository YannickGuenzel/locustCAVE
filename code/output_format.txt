Output format for each of N frames and n virtual animals.
xR/yR	:	position of the real animal in the virtual world
xV1/yV1 :	position of the first virtual animal in the virtual world 
[frame1, xR, yR, xV1, yV1, .... ,xVn, yVn]
[frame2, xR, yR, xV1, yV1, .... ,xVn, yVn]
[                  . . .                 ]
[frameN, xR, yR, xV1, yV1, .... ,xVn, yVn]
